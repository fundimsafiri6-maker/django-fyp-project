❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a complaint?

To submit a complaint, navigate to the "Submit Complaint" section from your dashboard. Fill in the title and detailed description of your complaint, then click "Submit". Your complaint will be reviewed and assigned to the appropriate staff member for handling.

How long does it take to resolve a complaint?

Resolution time varies depending on the complexity of your complaint. Most complaints are addressed within 5-7 working days. You can track the status of your complaint in real-time through your dashboard.

Can I edit my complaint after submission?

Once a complaint is submitted, you cannot edit it directly. However, you can provide additional information by contacting the assigned staff member through the complaint detail page or using the support system.

How does the AI classification work?

Our AI system categorizes complaints automatically based on keywords and context. This helps route your complaint to the correct department quickly. The AI also analyzes sentiment to identify urgent issues that need immediate attention.

Is my complaint confidential?

Yes, all complaints are treated with confidentiality. Only authorized staff members have access to your complaint details. Your privacy is protected at all times in accordance with our data protection policies.
